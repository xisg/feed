# LLVM Developer Meeting 2010

The [2010 edition](http://www.llvm.org/devmtg/2010-11/) of the [LLVM Developer's Meeting](http://www.llvm.org/devmtg/) has come and gone, and it was a great event. It featured 17 great talks, several BOF sessions, and lots of informal discussions between all sorts of people interested in LLVM and its subprojects. Lang Hames wrote up a great summary of [his experience at the meeting](http://google-opensource.blogspot.com/2010/12/notes-from-2010-llvm-developers-meeting.html).

Unfortunately, there was more people who wanted to attend than we were able to accomodate, but fortunately the slides and videos of all the talks are [available the web page](http://www.llvm.org/devmtg/2010-11/). Many thanks go our to our sponsors: Apple, QuIC, Google, Qualcomm.

Happy holidays,

-Chris
