# Euro LLVM 2013 in Paris

In two weeks, the [2013 edition of the Euro LLVM](http://llvm.org/devmtg/2013-04/) conference will start in historic center of Paris, France.  The schedule has just been published.  Most of different aspects of the LLVM infrastructure will present at the event.  See below for the full details!
