# Generative AI Strategy

I had a lot of fun preparing the talk: *“Leadership needs us to do generative AI. What do we do?”* for [Fully Connected](https://fullyconnected.com/). The idea for the talk came from many conversations I’ve had recently with friends who need to figure out their generative AI strategy, but aren’t sure what exactly to do.

This talk is a simple framework to explore what to do with generative AI. Many ideas are still being fleshed out. I hope to convert this into a proper post when I have more time. In the meantime, I’d love to hear from your experience through this process.
