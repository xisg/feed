### Basic Topics in Experimental Computer Science

This paper is a start on a report on experimental computer science (and its relationship to theoretical and applied computer science).
Basic Topics in Experimental Computer Science is an introduction to a report on this topic covering basic experimental research neglected in Academic Careers for Experimental Computer Scientists and Engineers. The sections of the report need to be written, and undoubtedly new topics for sections will prove important.

Tentatively, I will be the editor, but if someone who knows my deficiencies in that respect will volunteer, I'll defer.
