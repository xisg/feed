# [$] An overlayfs update

In a shortened session in the filesystem track at the 2026 [Linux Storage,\
Filesystem, Memory Management, and BPF Summit](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/lsfmmbpf/), Amir Goldstein gave an
update on the [overlayfs\
union filesystem](https://docs.kernel.org/filesystems/overlayfs.html). There are some new features over the last few years
that he wanted to mention, along with looking at the status of nesting
overlayfs layers. The [composefs use case](https://lwn.net/Articles/922851/)
that was [discussed at the summit in 2023](https://lwn.net/Articles/933616/)
has led to some interesting changes to overlayfs.
