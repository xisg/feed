# Eight new stable kernels for Friday

Greg Kroah-Hartman has announced the release of the [7.1.1](https://lwn.net/Articles/1078591/), [7.0.13](https://lwn.net/Articles/1078592/), [6.18.36](https://lwn.net/Articles/1078593/), [6.12.94](https://lwn.net/Articles/1078594/), [6.6.143](https://lwn.net/Articles/1078595/), [6.1.176](https://lwn.net/Articles/1078596/), [5.15.210](https://lwn.net/Articles/1078597/), and [5.10.259](https://lwn.net/Articles/1078598/) stable kernels. As usual, each contains important fixes. Users are advised to upgrade.
