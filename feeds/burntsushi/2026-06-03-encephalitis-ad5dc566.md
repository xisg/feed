# Encephalitis

I was recently diagnosed with [anti-NMDA receptor encephalitis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anti-NMDA_receptor_encephalitis). It is
an autoimmune disorder where your body’s normally helpful antibodies
[start acting strangely](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anti-NMDA_receptor_encephalitis#Pathophysiology). This leads to inflammation in the brain. This
blog briefly discusses some of my experience and prognosis.

**Target audience**: Anyone relying on my work for their own projects.
