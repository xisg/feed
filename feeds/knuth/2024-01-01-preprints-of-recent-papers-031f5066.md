# Preprints of Recent Papers

Electronic approximations to most of the papers I've written since 1990 can be downloaded by clicking appropriate links below. In general, these files are in plain TeX format, and they represent the initial form of the paper before copy editing and other improvements made by the publishers.

To typeset many of these files you will need [Macros for illustrations `picmac.tex` (2458 bytes)](https://www-cs-faculty.stanford.edu/~knuth/papers/picmac.tex.gz). Sometimes you also need to generate illustrations with the [MetaPost](http://cm.bell-labs.com/who/hobby/MetaPost.html) source files provided. The published papers often include illustrations that are not available in electronic form.

Of course users of these files should not violate the copyrights of the publishers.

These preprints are classified into four categories:

- [Refereed papers](#ref)
- [Unrefereed papers](#unref)
- [Informal notes](#unpub)
- [Miscellaneous](#misc)

In each group the papers are listed in reverse chronological order. Identification numbers like P156 and Q133 refer to my [list of publications](https://www-cs-faculty.stanford.edu/~knuth/vita.html), where complete bibliographic data can be found.
